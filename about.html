♥ OUR STORY ♥


Sweety n Side Cafe started with the simple vision of building a space to share beautiful and delicious desserts. We want our visitors to come and experience unique flavors from around the world, in a setting that is not only welcoming but one you would want to revisit time and time again.

No matter the occasion, we will handcraft your order with care and strive to make your visit something special.
